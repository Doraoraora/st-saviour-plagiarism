Draft #1 of College Essay

If it weren’t for Mamma Mia, I would most likely be bald. 
As dramatic as it sounds, it’s true. 
The growth of not only my hair, but my character reflects a time of self-discovery, 
growth of community, and self-judgement. No matter the stage of my life, 
the easiest way to describe my past self is depersonalized. 
I don’t recognize myself before theater or without curls on my head. 
Like the wavy hair I had sadly grown accustomed to, my life was like a watered down curl cream… 
it’s not going to help anyone in its current state. 
The life of an honors and AP student with no passion is like the life of a balloon: 
you will slowly deflate, until you’re eventually nothing—that was me. 

                                        -- Doraly Baez-Andujar, 2025
