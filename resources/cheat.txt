Draft #2 of College Essay Intro

If it weren’t for Mamma Mia, I would most likely be bald. 
As dramatic as it sounds, it’s true. 
No matter the stage of my life, I felt detached from myself. 
I don’t recognize the girl I was before theater, without curls on my head, 
or the realization of the importance of my Dominican heritage. 
The life of an honors and AP student with no passion is like wash day without a diffuser: 
your hair will slowly deflate as it dries and you feel it was all for nothing. 
Like the wavy hair I had sadly grown accustomed to, my life was like a watered down curl cream, 
 it’s not going to help anyone in its current state.

                                                -- Doraly Baez-Andujar, 2025
